Ethoscope Hardware

This page explains what type of hardware is needed to set-up a minimal working platform.

Overview

A FIGURE HERE!!! In order to work, the platform must contain:

  • A Node. This is a regular computer. It allows to configure (stop/start/...) Ethoscopes and fetches their data.
  • A Router. Available commercially. It is required to establish a wireless connection between Node and Ethoscopes.
  • Ethoscopes. These are custom made devices. They acquire video, analyse behaviour in real time and store results.
The real power of the ethoscopes resides in being able to 3d print customised arenas. In addition real time analysis will allow users to interact with animals according to their behaviour, which can be achieved thanks to optional modules.

Ethoscope

The Ethoscope devices are the most important part of the platform. Indeed, they are customisable standalone tracking devices. One solution to get Ethoscopes is to contract polygonal tree, our commercial partner. It is also perfectly feasible to 3d print/build your own by following the online hardware documentation.

Arenas

Ethoscopes can fit a variety of 3d printed arenas. Examples are available on the hardware repository. Their are also blank templates that can be derived in order to generate novel arenas.
