Ethoscope Hardware

This page explains what type of hardware is needed to set-up a minimal working platform.

Overview

A FIGURE HERE!!! In order to work, the platform must contain:

  • A Node. This is a regular computer. It allows to configure (stop/start/...) Ethoscopes and fetches their data.
  • A Router. Available commercially. It is requiered to establish a wireless connection between Node and Ethoscopes.
  • Ethoscopes. These are custom made devices. They acquiere video, analyse behaviour in real time and store results.
The real power of the ethoscopes resides in being able to 3d print customised arenas. In addition real time analysis will allow users to interact with animals according to their behaviour, which can be accheived thanks to optional modules.
