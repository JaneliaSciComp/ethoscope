Authors

The Ethoscope platform is a BBSRC funded project, which was primarily developed to analyse sleep in fruit fly in the Gilestro lab. All people in Gilestro lab have widely contributed to this project by testing early versions and reporting issues. Specifically:

  • Quentin Geissmann
    • is the main developer and maintainer of the platform.
  • Luis Garcia
    • has been highly involved in the design of the hardware and user interface.
  • Giorgio Gilestro
    • is the principal investigator and leader of the project.
